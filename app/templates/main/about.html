About DermAssist-AI

An explainable AI clinical decision support system for preliminary skin condition screening.

What it does
  • Classifies uploaded skin images into Acne, Eczema, Psoriasis, Melanoma-like, or Normal.
  • Generates Grad-CAM heatmaps highlighting suspicious regions.
  • Produces downloadable PDF reports with findings and recommendations.
  • Saves history for registered users.
Technology

Built with Python / Flask, PyTorch EfficientNet-B0, Grad-CAM, and Bootstrap 5. Training data sourced from ISIC 2019/2020 and DermNet NZ.

Disclaimer: DermAssist-AI is a screening aid for educational purposes only. It does not replace a certified dermatologist. Always seek professional medical advice.
